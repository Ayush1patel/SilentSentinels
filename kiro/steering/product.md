## Mission Statement

**Silent Sentinel exists to protect those who cannot hear danger.**

We believe that safety should not depend on the ability to hear. Our AI-powered emergency detection system transforms sound into sight, giving deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals the same reaction time as everyone else.

## Target Users

| Persona | Needs |
|---------|-------|
| **Deaf individuals** | Visual alerts, haptic feedback, no audio reliance |
| **Hard of hearing** | Amplified visual cues, customizable sensitivity |
| **Parents of deaf children** | Remote monitoring, mobile alerts |
| **Caregivers** | Dashboard monitoring, history logs |
| **First responders** | Reliable detection, minimal false alarms |

---

## Success Criteria

1. **Detection Accuracy:** >98% for critical sounds (gunshots, fire alarms, glass breaking)
2. **False Positive Rate:** <5% after AI verification
3. **Alert Latency:** <500ms from sound to visual alert
4. **Accessibility Score:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliant
5. **User Confidence:** Zero missed emergencies in testing
